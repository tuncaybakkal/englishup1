Kural Soruları
1 – How is the regular past tense formed?
By adding -ed to the base form. (-ed eklenerek)
2 – How is the past tense formed when the verb ends in a consonant + -y?
Drop the y and add -ied. (y düşer, -ied eklenir)
3 – When do we double the final consonant?
When there is a short vowel sound followed by a single consonant. (e.g. stop→stopped, plan→planned)

s.32 Practice – Discussing Grammar
Alıştırma 1 – Cümle çiftleri arasındaki fark nedir?
1a — When we arrived, she was making coffee.
She was in the process of making coffee when they arrived. (Devam eden eylem — Past Continuous)
1b — When we arrived, she made coffee.
They arrived first, and then she made coffee. (Ardışık tamamlanan eylem — Past Simple)
2a — What were you doing when you saw the accident?
Asks what activity you were doing at the time the accident happened.
2b — What did you do when you saw the accident?
Asks what action you took after the accident happened.

s.38 Writing – Picture Stories / Narrative Writing

Alıştırma 2 – Aunt Camilla's Portrait (Resimlerden hikâye yazın)
Örnek hikâye (kendim yazdım)
Picture 1: My Aunt Camilla was old and very vain. One day she looked in the mirror and suddenly decided that she wanted a portrait of herself. Immediately, she contacted the world-famous portrait painter, Rolf Unwin. Picture 2: Twice a week she visited his studio. Rolf slowly painted her portrait. He didn't want her to see the picture until it was finished. Picture 3: Finally the portrait was ready. My aunt was excited and hurried to the studio. The portrait looked exactly like my aunt. Unfortunately she was furious and refused to accept it. She ordered him to paint a new one! Picture 4: This time my aunt didn't visit the studio. After another few months the portrait was ready and my aunt came to see it. The face was a beautiful, young girl. It didn't look like my aunt at all – but she absolutely loved it. She paid Rolf a fortune!
