A Good News Story – Alıştırma 1
Why is this a good news story?
Because a man helped to save a penguin and the penguin travels back to see him.
Alıştırma 2 – Kim/Ne/Nerede?
Who is João Pereira de Souza? Who is Dindim?
João is a retired bricklayer who still works part-time as a fisherman. Dindim is a penguin.
Where did João find Dindim?
On the beach near his home.
Where does Dindim swim to every year?
He swims to visit João every year (2,500 miles to Argentina or Chile, then back).

Alıştırma 1 – Discussing Grammar: Fark nedir?
1aWhen we arrived, she was making coffee.
She was in the process of making coffee when they arrived. (devam eden eylem)
1bWhen we arrived, she made coffee.
They arrived first, and then she made coffee. (tamamlanan eylem)
2aWhat were you doing when you saw the accident?
Asks what activity you were doing at the time the accident happened.
2bWhat did you do when you saw the accident?
Asks what action you took after the accident happened.

Reading & Listening – A Spy Story (James Bond)
Alıştırma 4 – Sorular & Cevaplar
1Name the people in the pictures. Where are they?
James Bond, Mary Goodnight, Scaramanga – they are in a hotel room.
2How did Mary Goodnight get into the room?
Through the window.
3Why did she come to James Bond?
To give him an important message from headquarters – a top KGB man (Hendriks) is staying in the same hotel and is looking for him, and so is Hendriks' gunman, Scaramanga.
4Where did they go to talk?
The bathroom.
5What did Scaramanga say? What did he do?
'This is not your lucky day, Mr Bond. Come here both of you and put your hands up.' He turned on the lights and pointed his golden gun at James Bond.

Writing – Picture Stories / Narrative Writing
Alıştırma 1 – Sıfat ve Zarfları Doğru Yere Koyun
1 A dog jumped up at me. (large, suddenly)
A large dog suddenly jumped up at me. / Suddenly, a large dog jumped up at me.
2 Thank you for your invitation. I can't come. (kind, unfortunately)
Thank you for your kind invitation. Unfortunately, I can't come. / I can't come, unfortunately.
3 I got out of bed and went to make a cup of tea. (downstairs, nice)
I got out of bed and went downstairs to make a nice cup of tea.
4 We had a meal and we went to the theatre. (lovely, then)
We had a lovely meal and then we went to the theatre.
5 I was sitting at home when something happened. (last Thursday evening, very strange)
I was sitting at home last Thursday evening when something very strange happened.
Last Thursday evening, I was sitting at home when something very strange happened.
6 He's got three sisters, and I've got three older sisters. (older, too)
He's got three older sisters, and I've got three older sisters, too.
7 There's a programme on TV tonight that I want to see. (interesting, very much)
There's an interesting programme on TV tonight that I want to see very much. / …that I very much want to see.
8 I worked all week. (really hard, last)
I worked really hard all last week. / Last week, I worked really hard.
