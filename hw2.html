People say we're starting to look more different now… We're getting older… / Dylan's studying Drama in Manchester and I'm doing Physics in Leeds. … we're living very different lives.

Practice
Talking about you – Alıştırma 1
have ve have got'ın soru, olumsuz ve kısa cevapta farkı nedir?
You don't use the auxiliary do in questions, short answers, and negatives with have got. (Have you got…? / Yes, I have. | Do you have…? / Yes, I do.)

Listening – Getting on with the Neighbours
Alıştırma 2 – "Good fences make good neighbours" ne anlama gelir?
It could be interpreted in various ways. One way: neighbours should respect each other's property and boundaries. A less literal interpretation: keep some distance and don't become too friendly with your neighbour.

1Where is Alfie's flat?

Writing – Linking Words
but / although / however – Kural Özeti
but → iki cümleyi birleştirir; ikinci cümleden önce gelir (virgülle). although → cümle başında veya ortasında kullanılabilir; şaşırtıcı bir zıtlık belirtir. however → iki ayrı cümleyi bağlar; nokta + However, … şeklinde.
Alıştırma 3 – Cümle Dönüştürme
1 I love ice cream. I try not to eat it too often.
I love ice cream, but I try not to eat it too often.
Although I love ice cream, I try not to eat it too often.
I try not to eat ice cream too often, although I love it.
I love ice cream. However, I try not to eat it too often.
2 Alex is a good friend. We don't have a lot in common.
Alex is a good friend, but we don't have a lot in common.
Although Alex is a good friend, we don't have a lot in common.
Alex is a good friend, although we don't have a lot in common.
Alex is a good friend. However, we don't have a lot in common.
3 I live near Zoe. I don't see her very often.
I live near Zoe, but I don't see her very often.
Although I live near Zoe, I don't see her very often.
I don't see Zoe very often, although I live near her.
I live near Zoe. However, I don't see her very often.
4 It rained a lot. We had a really good holiday.
It rained a lot, but we had a really good holiday.
Although it rained a lot, we had a really good holiday.
We had a really good holiday, although it rained a lot.
It rained a lot. However, we had a really good holiday.
so / because – Kural Özeti
so → önce sebep, sonra sonuç (Cause → Result). because → önce sonuç, sonra sebep (Result → Cause).
Alıştırma 5 – so / because ile Dönüştürme
1
I don't like broccoli, so I don't eat it.
I don't eat broccoli because I don't like it.
2
She was tired, so she went home.
She went home because she was tired.
3
The train was very expensive, so we went by bus.
We went by bus because the train was very expensive.
4
It started to rain, so we had to stop the tennis match.
We had to stop the tennis match because it started to rain.
5
My grandmother's ill, so I'm going to visit her.
I'm going to visit my grandmother because she's ill.
6
I really like the teacher, so I enjoy history lessons.
I enjoy history lessons because I really like the teacher.
